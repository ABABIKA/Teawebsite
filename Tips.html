Tea Brewing Tips

Helpful Tips for Making the Perfect Cup of Tea

Use fresh, filtered water for the best taste.

Heat water to the appropriate temperature for your tea type.

Steep tea for the right amount of time to avoid bitterness.

Use high-quality tea leaves or tea bags for better flavor.

Add honey, lemon, or milk to enhance taste, if desired.

Store tea properly in an airtight container away from light and moisture.

Types of Tea

Green Tea - Rich in antioxidants and has a fresh, grassy flavor.

Black Tea - Bold and robust flavor, great for a morning boost.

Herbal Tea - Caffeine-free and soothing with a variety of flavors.

Oolong Tea - A perfect balance between green and black tea.
